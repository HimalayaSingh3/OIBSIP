Virat Kohli - A Cricket Legend

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of the sport.

Biography

Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, Virat Kohli showed his potential at a young age. He led India to victory in the 2008 Under-19 World Cup and soon debuted for the senior team.
